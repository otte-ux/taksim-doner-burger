## Hero video — to source (free, commercial-use)

The hero `<video>` expects `assets/video/hero-1080.{webm,mp4}` (generated by `fetch-media.sh`).
Until then the hero shows the poster image (which is the intended LCP anyway). Suggested
free-license clips of a döner spit / sizzling grill:

- **Pexels Videos** — https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/doner/ (Pexels License)
- **Mixkit** — https://mixkit.co/free-stock-video/food/ (Mixkit License)
- **Coverr** — https://coverr.co/s?q=grill (Coverr License)

Download one clip, drop it in `scripts/` as `hero-raw.mp4`, and run `fetch-media.sh` to trim +
encode it. Record the exact clip URL + author + license here before shipping.

## Fonts

  `assets/fonts/` and swap the `<link>` for an `@font-face` block — see README.
